Welcome to Himachal Pradesh

Explore the beauty of hill stations, adventure activities, and monasteries in Himachal Pradesh.

Places to Visit

  • Manali: Known for its adventure sports and snow-capped mountains.
  • Shimla: Famous for colonial architecture and scenic views.
  • Spiti Valley: A remote, arid region known for its landscapes and Buddhist monasteries.
  • Kullu: Known for its trekking and scenic beauty.
  • Dharamsala: A spiritual haven and home to the Dalai Lama.
